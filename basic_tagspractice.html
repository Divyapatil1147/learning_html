Dagdusheth Ganpati

The Dagadusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to the Hindu god Ganesha (Ganapati) in the city of Pune, India. The temple is visited by over one hundred thousand devotees every year.[1] A large number of devotees visit the temple during the annual ten-day public Ganeshotsav festival.[2] The main Ganesha idol is insured for a sum of ₹10 million (US$100,000).[3] It celebrated 132 years of its Ganeshotsav festival in 2024.[4]
