About Us

The Government of Maharashtra accorded approval to the Sanstha to establish an Engineering College in 2010, followed by a Polytechnic College in 2011. Consequently, Padm. Dr. V. B. Kolte College of Engineering and Polytechnic, Malkapur came into existence on 1st July 2010. During the last 15 years of operation, the institute has shown remarkable growth both qualitatively and quantitatively through 4 Undergraduate and Postgraduate engineering programs.

As a result of adopting best practices, the institute has received ISO certification, NBA (Tier I) accreditation, NAAC reaccreditation, and proudly holds the prestigious IEI Gold Award. Furthermore, the institute is recognized under section 12A by UGC, reflecting its strong academic and regulatory standing.Read More...The institute strives to impart knowledge and skills to transform young minds into effective engineers rather than merely engineering graduates. Collaborative study with industries and research institutes within the country and abroad empowers our students to be accepted at global standards.
